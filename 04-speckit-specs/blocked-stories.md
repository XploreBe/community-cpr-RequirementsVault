# Blocked stories

No Phase 1 backlog items are blocked. All six dispatcher-web stories, all six volunteer-app stories, and all three backend-api enablers are Ready or Ready (conditional) — see [[00-index]].

Two Phase 1 items are Ready under a stated assumption rather than fully unconditional:
- **US-101 — Sign up with tier** (volunteer-app) — conditional on OQ-001 (final volunteer tier breakdown).
- **US-104 — Navigate to the scene** (volunteer-app) — conditional on OQ-006 (navigation provider conflict between the brief and the architecture diagram).

Neither is "blocked" in the sense of having no spec — both have a spec file, built under the stated assumption, with the assumption called out in the spec's "Unresolved" section. If either open question resolves differently than assumed, the corresponding spec (and possibly the story) needs rework, not just a config change.

This file will be populated with genuinely blocked items once Phase 2 stories are drafted — most of the open questions in 01-requirements-structured-v1.md §5 (OQ-003, OQ-004, OQ-005, OQ-011, etc.) will likely block specific Phase 2 stories outright.
